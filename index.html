How Does Style Shifter Work?

Seamless e-commerce integration: We provide plugins and APIs compatible
with Shopify, Magento, WooCommerce, and more, ensuring hassle-free onboarding process.

Virtual Try-On: A seamless user experience

1- Minimal User Input:

Users simply upload a single full-body image, making the process quick and effortless.

2- AI Processing:

Our advanced AI technology analyzes the user’s body shape, pose, and proportions to generate an accurate product representation.

3- See Visuals:

The AI creates a realistic virtual try-on, showcasing exactly how the selected garment will look on the user’s image. This interactive experience helps shoppers make confident purchase decisions.

Size Recommendation: Accurate and personalized

1- Minimal User Input:

Users provide basic details such as height, weight, and a full-body image.

2- AI Analysis:

Our computer vision technology processes the uploaded image to extract detailed body measurements, ensuring precision in size recommendations.

3- Precise Recommendations:

The system compares the user’s measurements with garment specifications to provide size suggestions and personalized fit advice, reducing the likelihood of sizing errors and returns.
